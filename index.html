Ratnesh Kr. Joshi

I am a Ph.D. Scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna, where I have recently submitted my thesis. My research focuses on Explainability in Natural Language Generation, with the goal of making conversational systems more transparent, faithful, and trustworthy.

My work centers on building advanced Conversational AI systems, with a focus on Agentic AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). I am passionate about developing intelligent agents with sophisticated capabilities like influencing and negotiation, as demonstrated in my research on persona-aware dialogue models and sustainability-focused chatbots.

I have gained valuable industry experience through internships at Samsung Research, where I worked on proprietary RAG systems, and at Accenture Labs, where I contributed to a conversational agent designed to promote sustainability through behavioral influencing.
